# Chinese Medical Device Standards (GB/YY)

China's medical device standards are published by **SAC** (Standardization Administration of China) and **NMPA**:
- **GB** — Mandatory national standards
- **YY** — Industry standards for medical devices
- **YY/T** — Recommended industry standards

::: tip Source
[openstd.samr.gov.cn](https://openstd.samr.gov.cn) — Official standards database
:::
