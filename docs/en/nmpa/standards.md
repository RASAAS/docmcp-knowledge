# Chinese Medical Device Standards (GB / YY)

China's medical device standards system consists of national standards and industry standards:

| Prefix | Type | Description |
|--------|------|-------------|
| **GB** | Mandatory national standard | Published by SAC, applicable to all medical devices |
| **GB/T** | Recommended national standard | Voluntary adoption |
| **YY** | Mandatory industry standard | Published by NMPA, specific to medical device industry |
| **YY/T** | Recommended industry standard | Voluntary adoption |

## Standards Library Overview

As of May 2026, our standards database contains:

| Metric | Count |
|--------|-------|
| Total standards | **2,100** |
| GB series | 334 |
| YY series | 1,766 |
| Currently active | 1,896 |
| Upcoming (not yet effective) | 204 |

Standards are organized into two domains:
- **General Technical Domain** (228 standards): Foundational standards applicable to most medical devices
- **Professional Technical Domain** (1,872 standards): Product-specific standards for particular device categories
