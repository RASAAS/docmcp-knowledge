### Abbott Diabetes Care Removes Certain FreeStyle Libre 3 and FreeStyle Libre 2 Sensors - FDA Recall

**2026-07-01** | Australia TGA | Safety Communication | !!! HIGH

The FDA announced that Abbott Diabetes Care is recalling specific lots of FreeStyle Libre 3 and FreeStyle Libre 2 continuous glucose monitoring sensors due to a risk of the sensor battery swelling or overheating, which may cause inaccurate glucose readings or burns. This is a Class I recall, the most serious type, indicating a reasonable probability of serious harm or death. Users should immediately check affected lot numbers posted on the FDA and Abbott websites, stop using recalled sensors, and contact Abbott for a free replacement.

### FDA MedWatch: Safety Information and Adverse Event Reporting Program

**2026-07-01** | Australia TGA | Regulation Update | !! MEDIUM
