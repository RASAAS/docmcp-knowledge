### PMDA Assessments for Registered Certification Bodies (QMS/GMP/GCTP)

**2026-07-01** | Japan PMDA | Regulation Update | !! MEDIUM

The PMDA conducts routine assessments of registered certification bodies (RCBs) that perform conformity inspections for medical devices and IVDs under Japan's Pharmaceutical and Medical Device Act. These evaluations verify that RCBs maintain proper competence, impartiality, and adherence to QMS, GMP, and GCTP requirements. Manufacturers using RCB certifications should monitor these oversight activities, as they can affect certificate validity and recognition.
