### PMDA Safety Alert: Precautions in Handling of Tracheostomy Tubes (No. 2) – Risk of Inner Cannula Dislodgement

**2026-07-01** | Japan PMDA | Safety Communication | !!! HIGH

The Japanese PMDA issued a second safety alert (No. 2) on tracheostomy tubes, warning that the inner cannula can inadvertently dislodge from the outer cannula during suctioning, coughing, or patient movement, potentially causing airway obstruction and oxygen desaturation. Healthcare professionals are advised to always check the secure engagement of the inner and outer cannulas before and after procedures, consider using tubes without detachable inner cannulas for high-risk patients, and ensure proper fixation. Manufacturers are requested to review and enhance their instructions for use and labeling to prevent such incidents.
