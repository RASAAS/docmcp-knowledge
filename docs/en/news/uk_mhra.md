### Class 3 Recall: Flucloxacillin 500mg Capsules – Incorrect Patient Leaflet (Flamingo Pharma UK Ltd)

**2026-06-30** | UK MHRA | Safety Communication | ! LOW

Flamingo Pharma UK Ltd has informed the MHRA that specific batches of Flucloxacillin Capsules BP 500mg contain the Patient Information Leaflet (PIL) for Amoxicillin 500mg Capsules instead of the correct Flucloxacillin PIL. This Class 3 recall requires healthcare professionals and wholesalers to check stock, quarantine affected packs, and return them to the supplier. The defect poses a low risk to patient safety, but incorrect dosing information could lead to confusion.
