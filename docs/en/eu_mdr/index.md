# EU Medical Device Regulation (EU MDR 2017/745)

The EU MDR 2017/745 entered into full application on **26 May 2021**, replacing MDD 93/42/EEC and AIMDD 90/385/EEC.

## Structure

- **[Regulations](./regulations)** — Full text and amendments
- **[Harmonised Standards](./standards)** — OJ-listed standards
- **[MDCG Guidance](./mdcg)** — Medical Device Coordination Group documents
- **[TEAM-NB Position Papers](./team_nb)** — Notified Body position papers
