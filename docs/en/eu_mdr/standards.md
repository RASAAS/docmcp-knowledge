# Harmonised Standards under EU MDR

Harmonised standards published in the **Official Journal of the EU (OJ)** give presumption of conformity with EU MDR requirements.

## Risk Management
| Standard | Title |
|----------|-------|
| EN ISO 14971:2019 | Medical devices — Risk management |
| EN ISO 24971:2020 | Guidance on ISO 14971 application |

## Software
| Standard | Title |
|----------|-------|
| EN IEC 62304:2006+A1:2015 | Medical device software lifecycle |
| EN IEC 81001-5-1:2021 | Health software cybersecurity |

## Biocompatibility
| Standard | Title |
|----------|-------|
| EN ISO 10993-1:2018 | Biological evaluation — Part 1 |
| EN ISO 10993-5:2009 | Cytotoxicity tests |
| EN ISO 10993-18:2020 | Chemical characterization |

## Electrical Safety
| Standard | Title |
|----------|-------|
| EN IEC 60601-1:2006+A1:2013 | Medical electrical equipment — General |
| EN IEC 60601-1-2:2015 | EMC requirements |
| EN IEC 60601-1-6:2010+A1:2015 | Usability |

## Sterilization
| Standard | Title |
|----------|-------|
| EN ISO 11135:2014 | EO sterilization |
| EN ISO 11137 series | Radiation sterilization |
| EN ISO 17665 series | Moist heat sterilization |

## Clinical Evaluation
| Standard | Title |
|----------|-------|
| EN ISO 14155:2020 | Clinical investigation of medical devices |
