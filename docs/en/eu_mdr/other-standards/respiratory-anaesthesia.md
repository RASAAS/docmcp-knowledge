| ISO 80601-2-12:2020 | Medical electrical equipment - Part 2-12: Particular requirements for the basic safety and essential performance of critical care ventilators | active | 1, 3, 5, 9, 14 | [Link](https://www.iso.org/standard/72069.html) |
