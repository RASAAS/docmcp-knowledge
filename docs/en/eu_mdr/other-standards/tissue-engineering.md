| ISO 13022:2012 | Medical products containing viable human cells - Application of risk management and requirements for processing practices | active | 1, 3, 5, 10 | [Link](https://www.iso.org/standard/52452.html) |
| ISO/TS 22911:2024 | Dentistry - Preclinical evaluation of dental implant systems - Animal test methods | active | 1, 3, 5, 10 | [Link](https://www.iso.org/standard/83574.html) |
| ASTM F2150-19 | Standard Guide for Characterization and Testing of Biomaterial Scaffolds Used in Tissue-Engineered Medical Products | active | 1, 3, 5, 10 | [Link](https://www.astm.org/f2150-19.html) |
| ASTM F2312-11(2020) | Standard Terminology Relating to Tissue Engineered Medical Products | active | 1, 3 | [Link](https://www.astm.org/f2312-11r2020.html) |
