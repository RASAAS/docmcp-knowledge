# Biocompatibility (Non-Harmonised)

This category contains **15** standards.

::: tip Note on Harmonisation Status
Standards listed here are widely used in the medical device industry for biocompatibility evaluation but are **NOT listed in the EU Official Journal** for presumption of conformity under MDR. For officially harmonised biocompatibility standards (8 standards from ISO 10993 Parts 4, 9, 10, 12, 15, 17, 18, 23), see [Harmonised Standards - Biocompatibility](/en/eu_mdr/standards/biocompatibility).
:::
