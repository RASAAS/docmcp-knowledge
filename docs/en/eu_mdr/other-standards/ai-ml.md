## Regulatory Context

The EU MDR requires that medical devices incorporating AI/ML comply with GSPR 14 (software as a device) and GSPR 17 (electronic programmable systems). The EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689) additionally classifies most AI-based medical devices as **high-risk AI systems** requiring conformity assessment.

Key regulatory guidance:
- **MDCG 2021-24 rev.1**: Guidance on classification of medical devices (AI/ML)
- **MDCG 2024-7**: MDR/IVDR and the AI Act
- **FDA**: Predetermined Change Control Plan (PCCP) for ML-enabled devices
