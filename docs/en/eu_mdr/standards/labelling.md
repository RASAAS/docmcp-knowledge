# Harmonised Standards — Labelling and Instructions for Use

**Official Source**: [EC Health — Harmonised Standards](https://health.ec.europa.eu/medical-devices-topics-interest/harmonised-standards_en) | Based on CID (EU) 2021/1182 and amendment CID 2024/815

## Standards List

| Standard | Title | GSPR Reference | Status |
|----------|-------|---------------|--------|
| **EN ISO 15223-1:2021** | Medical devices — Symbols to be used with information to be supplied by the manufacturer — Part 1: General requirements | GSPR 23 | Current |
| **EN ISO 20417:2021** | Medical devices — Information to be supplied by the manufacturer | GSPR 23 | Current |

## Mandatory Label Information (EU MDR Article 23)

- Manufacturer name and registered address
- Device name or trade name
- Batch number (LOT) or serial number (SN)
- UDI carrier (barcode/QR code)
- Date of manufacture and/or expiry date
- Sterility indication (if applicable)
