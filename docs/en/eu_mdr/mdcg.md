# MDCG Guidance Documents

The **Medical Device Coordination Group (MDCG)** consists of representatives of EU Member States and provides guidance on MDR/IVDR implementation.

::: tip Source
[EC — MDCG Endorsed Documents](https://health.ec.europa.eu/medical-devices-sector/new-regulations/guidance-mdcg-endorsed-documents-and-other-guidance_en)
:::
