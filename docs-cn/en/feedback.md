# Continuous Development & User Feedback

Welcome to Reguverse Assistant. Our platform is dedicated to providing you with efficient, professional digital support for regulatory compliance. We are proud to serve a growing community of users across trial and paid subscription tiers.

## Under Active Development

To ensure you always have access to the latest and most advanced features, we are continuously upgrading and developing the assistant at a rapid pace. As new capabilities are introduced and AI models are fine-tuned, it is possible that occasional bugs may occur, or that certain edge-case scenarios may not fully meet your specific business requirements during this iterative phase.

## Your Feedback Matters

We understand that reliable service and a tailored experience are critical to your work. The Reguverse team considers real-world user feedback the core driving force behind our product evolution. We sincerely welcome and highly value all feedback from our users -- whether it concerns technical issues, optimization suggestions, or unmet feature needs.

If you encounter any product anomalies during use, or have constructive ideas regarding the AI assistant's interaction design or feature expansion, please do not hesitate to reach out through the channels below. Every piece of feedback helps Reguverse Assistant become more robust, and we will continue to optimize the system architecture based on the common needs of our user community.
