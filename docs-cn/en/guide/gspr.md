## Overview

The GSPR (General Safety and Performance Requirements) workflow systematically evaluates device compliance against EU MDR Annex I requirements, generating a complete GSPR compliance checklist.

## Annex I Structure

| Chapter | Requirements | Content |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| Chapter I | GSPR 1-9 | General requirements |
| Chapter II Part A | GSPR 10-16 | Chemical/physical/biological |
| Chapter II Part B | GSPR 17-22 | Software/cybersecurity/radiation |
| Chapter III | GSPR 23 | Labeling and instructions |

## Workflow Steps

1. **Step 1**: Chapter I (GSPR 1-9) -- General safety requirements
2. **Step 2**: Chapter II Part A (GSPR 10-16) -- Material and biological requirements
3. **Step 3**: Chapter II Part B (GSPR 17-22) -- Software and cybersecurity
4. **Step 4**: Chapter III (GSPR 23) -- Labeling requirements
5. **Step 5**: Final document assembly

## Intelligent Features

### Device Type Applicability

AI identifies the device archetype (SaMD / hardware-software / physical) and automatically determines clause applicability:
- Pure software: material/biological clauses marked N/A
- Physical devices: software/cybersecurity clauses marked N/A

### Cross-References

Automatically references data from CE and Risk Management tasks in the same project.

## Output

Landscape-format multi-column table with: GSPR No., General Requirement (full Annex I text), Comply (Y/NA), Method of Demonstration, Document Reference.
