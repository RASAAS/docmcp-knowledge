# Evidence Registry

::: info Available Plans
Pro / Max / CRO
:::

## Overview

The Evidence Registry manages and tracks all evidence sources used during clinical evaluation, establishing complete traceability from evidence to document sections.

## Features

- Unified management of literature, clinical data, and safety reports
- Evidence-to-CER/CEP chapter mapping
- Evidence validity and update status tracking
- Quick source location during NB audits

## Use Cases

- Notified Body audit preparation
- Internal QMS audits
- Evidence tracking during periodic CE updates
- Cross-project evidence reuse
