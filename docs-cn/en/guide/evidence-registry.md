# Evidence Registry

## Overview

The Evidence Registry manages and registers verification evidence for risk control measures, establishing traceability links between controls and their supporting evidence.

::: info Current Status
The Evidence Registry currently focuses on registering verification evidence for risk control measures in the risk management workflow. This feature is under continuous improvement.
:::

**Available on:** Pro / Max / CRO

## Features

### Evidence Registration

- Associate verification evidence with each risk control measure
- Supports multiple evidence types: test reports, standards conformity declarations, clinical data, literature references
- Records evidence source, date, and validity

### Traceability Management

- Establish control measure → evidence mapping
- Multiple evidence items per control measure
- Track evidence validity status

### Risk Management Integration

- Add evidence directly from the risk management panel for each control measure
- Evidence status reflected in the RCM matrix
